# Impact of a Jet Experiment (Fluid Mechanics)

This repository contains a laboratory report analysing the force produced when a water jet strikes a surface.

The experiment applies the momentum equation to predict the force produced by a jet impacting:

• a flat plate
• a hemispherical cup

Measured forces are compared with theoretical predictions to evaluate the accuracy of the momentum model.

## Topics Covered

Fluid momentum equation  
Mass flow rate and force relationship  
Experimental measurement of jet forces  
Comparison of theoretical and measured results
